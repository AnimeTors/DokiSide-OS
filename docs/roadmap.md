# **1. Introduction**
DokiSide OS is a cinematic Linux distribution inspired by the emotional worlds of *Doki Doki Literature Club* and *MiSide*.  
It blends pastel softness with cozy‑uncanny atmosphere, offering three full themes, preinstalled games, creator tools, and a multiverse‑style theme switcher.

This roadmap outlines the complete development plan from concept to release.

---

# **2. Project Vision**
DokiSide OS is designed to be:
- A **playable** universe (DDLC + MiSide Demo)  
- A **creatable** universe (Ren’Py, Unity, Godot, Unreal)  
- A **cinematic** universe (themes, animations, boot sequences)  
- A **cohesive** universe (DDLC, MiSide, Hybrid modes)
